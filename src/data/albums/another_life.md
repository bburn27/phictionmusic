Sometimes we hold onto the idea that a version of life exists where everything finally makes sense.
A different road. A different timing. A different place. A new you.

Maybe in **Another Life** we'd find everything we were searching for.
But would it still feel the same without the people, memories, and moments that made us who we are now?

Will we ever find our sense of belonging?
